Game Rules

How the Game Works

Each week, players pick the winners of NFL games and wager points on their choices. Correct picks earn the number of points wagered, while incorrect picks cost double that amount.

Every player must make at least five picks per week and has one “Double Up” to use. A Double Up doubles both the reward and the penalty of that pick, adding more risk and excitement.

Examples:

  • Pick the Eagles, wager 4 points, Eagles win → +4 points
  • Pick the Steelers, wager 5 points with a Double Up, Steelers lose → −20 points

Pick Deadlines

Picks are open and editable until the kickoff of each game. You must have a minimum of five picks to submit, but you can adjust your selections and wagers as often as you’d like before each game begins.
